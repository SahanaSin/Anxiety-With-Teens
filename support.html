Anxiety Disorders Overview Most Common Symptoms
Generalised anxiety disorder (GAD) GAD is the most common type of anxiety disorder.
  • All symptoms previously mentioned
Panic disorder Panic disorder means that you have regular panic attacks with no particular trigger..
  • Overwhelming sense of fear
  • Chest pain
  • Sweating or shivering
  • Dry mouth
  • Dizziness
  • Numbness/tingling sensation
Social anxiety disorder Social anxiety, also known as social phobia, is when you have an intense fear or dread of social situations. This can occur before, during or after an event.
  • Sweating
  • Fast heartbeat
  • Shaky voice
  • Blushing
Health anxiety Health anxiety is when you constantly worry that you are sick, or that you are going to get ill.
  • Constantly checking your body for signs of sickness
  • Asking others repeatedly if you are sick
  • Worry that advice from medical professionals are false
  • Obssesive around health-related information
Phobias A phobia is an overwhelming fear of an object, place, situation, feeling or animal.
  • Overwhelming fear
Agoraphobia Agoraphobia is a fear of being in places where it may be difficult to escape.
  • Heavy breathing when imagining a situation without an exit
Obsessive compulsive disorder (OCD) OCD is a long-lasting disorder where a person experiences engages in repetitive behaviors (compulsions) as an obsession.
  • Get temporarily relieved when doing a compulsion
  • Spend long periods of time on compulsions
